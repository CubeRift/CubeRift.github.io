Frequently Asked Questions

Is CodeVault completely free? ▶
Yes. Creating an account, uploading code, and organising your vault with folders is 100% free with no limits. The public browse page shows small ads to cover hosting costs — your private vault has no ads.
Who can see my uploaded code? ▶
Only you can see your private vault. Code only appears on the public browse page if it's tagged for public sharing. We never expose private gists without your action.
Can I delete my account and data? ▶
Yes. Send us an email with your username and we will remove your account credentials and all associated code from the system within 7 days.
What happens if I forget my password? ▶
Contact us with your username and we can reset your account. Since passwords are hashed we cannot retrieve the original, but we can create a new one for you after verifying your identity.
Can I request a specific lab program? ▶
Absolutely — use the contact form above and select "Code Request". We'll try to add it to the public library or point you to an existing snippet.
Is the code checked for correctness? ▶
Code in the public library is uploaded by students and is not formally reviewed or guaranteed to be error-free. Always test code before submitting it in your lab. Use it as a reference, not a copy-paste solution.
